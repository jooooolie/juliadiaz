Abstract City

2021, digital video, 28 seconds

I made this video for an assignment for ART-2 with abstract art as the topic. I decided to make a small trip to San Fransisco and noticed all the unique building structures/aspects of the city. I took video clips of each thing I saw and played with different colors and effects on Adobe Premiere Pro.
